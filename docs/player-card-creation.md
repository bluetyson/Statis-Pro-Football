The BIG PLAY DEFENSE 

In establishing the cards, do the **BIG PLAY defense card** first. Any team that wins **NINE or more games** gets a reading which enables it to "x" number of big plays per game per the 5th Edition Rules. As a rule, the Super Bowl team gets a rating of Home-4 and Road-4, unless it was a wild card team and then it must be subdued. This is a common sense measurement and is designed to explain why teams, such as Minnesota in 1987, were able to get through a so-so season to near the Super Bowl. Stats, alone, do not always explain, and these teams simply...
